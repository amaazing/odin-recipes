Soft-Boiled Egg

Barely cooked but warm and comforting

Soft-boiled eggs are an easy beginners recipe that pretty much anyone can make in a short amount of time.

Ingredients

  • 1 Egg
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions

  1. Fill saucepan around 2/3 full of water, bring to gentle boil.
  2. Lower the egg gently into the water
  3. Cook for 5-6 minutes
  4. Gently wash the egg with cold water, peel shell.
  5. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and enjoy!
