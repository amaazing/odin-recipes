Sauteed Apples

A sweet treat

This recipe can easily be altered to sautee any fruit at all.

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 1 pound aples cut into 10 pieces
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Heat butter in a large skillet
  2. When butter is melted, add apples and stir occasionally. Cook for 10 mins.
  3. Add sugar and cinnamon. Cook and stir until tender and glazed.
